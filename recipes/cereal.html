Cereal

Description

Cereal is a popular breakfast dish typically made from processed grains such as wheat, corn, oats, or rice. It is often served in a bowl with milk or yogurt and can be enjoyed hot or cold. Cereal comes in a wide variety of forms, including flakes, puffs, clusters, or shredded pieces, and is frequently sweetened or flavored with ingredients like sugar, honey, chocolate, or fruit. Many cereals are fortified with vitamins and minerals, making them a convenient and nutritious meal option. It can also be topped with fresh fruits, nuts, or seeds for added flavor and texture.

Cereal Recipe Steps

  1. Grab a Bowl and Cereal
  2. Pour the Cereal in the Bowl
  3. Pour the Milk
  4. Cut up Fruit
  5. Sprinkle Fruit on Top
  6. Eat with Spoon
